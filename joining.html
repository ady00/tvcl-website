How To Join

Check out the different ways to join below!

For any questions, feel free to email trivalleychessleague@gmail.com. Alternatively, you could join the TVCL Discord server and reach out to a moderator.

Join as an Individual

If you wish to gain membership as an individual player, sign up using this google form! The form will include details on our discord server, which will be used as the primary source of communication for all online activities. Individual players are allowed to compete in tournaments and take part in any events, such as Summer Camps.

https://forms.gle/pNxLoDxBjeMaYMtq7

Join as a School

Are you an officer of a chess club in the Tri-Valley and want your school to join? Send us an email with your club's information. Afterward, we can discuss more details and your school will be able to officially join as a member of the Tri-Valley Chess League. Members of your chess club would be able to participate in any and all TVCL events, and as a school you get to send up to 2 representatives to help coordinate and organize TVCL events.

Send an email to: trivalleychessleague@gmail.com
